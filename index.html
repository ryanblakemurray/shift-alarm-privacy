Privacy Policy

Shift Alarm — Shift Worker Calendar And Alarm

Shift Alarm is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your personal data should stay personal.

All of your shift schedules, alarm settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share any personal data beyond what is described below.

What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No personal information
  • No location data
  • No usage or behavioral data
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No data shared with third parties for marketing

Subscriptions

  • RevenueCat — We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your purchase transactions through Apple and receives an anonymous app user ID to track your subscription status. No personal information such as your name, email, or payment details is shared with RevenueCat. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.
  • Apple — All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. Shift Alarm never sees or stores your payment information.

Permissions

  • Notifications — Used solely to deliver your shift alarms. No data leaves your device.
  • AlarmKit (iOS 26+) — Used to schedule alarms that can break through Silent Mode and Do Not Disturb. No data is collected.

Data Storage

All app data (shift types, schedules, alarm configurations, and user preferences) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is included in your iCloud/iTunes backups if you have device backups enabled, but is never sent to any external server.

Children's Privacy

Shift Alarm does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be reflected on this page.
