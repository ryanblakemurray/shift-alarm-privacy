Privacy Policy

Shift Alarm - Shift Worker Calendar And Alarm

Your shift schedule, alarms, and preferences are stored on your device.

What We Collect

Shift Alarm collects limited app analytics to improve the app and troubleshoot issues.

  • Basic app usage events, such as screens viewed and feature usage
  • Subscription status events through RevenueCat
  • Session replay data through PostHog to help diagnose product issues and improve the app experience

How We Use It

  • To understand how the app is used
  • To monitor reliability, bugs, and failed flows
  • To manage subscriptions and restore purchases

What Stays On Your Device

Your shift schedule, alarm configuration, pay setup, bills, and preferences stay on your device unless included in your own Apple device backups.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat is used to manage subscriptions and purchase status.
  • Apple handles all App Store payments.
  • PostHog is used for first-party analytics and session replay.

Session Replay

Shift Alarm uses session replay to review app behavior and debug issues. Sensitive inputs and certain sensitive screens are masked in replay.
